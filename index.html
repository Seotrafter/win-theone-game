FAQ

Common Questions About Alien: Isolation

Find answers to the most frequently asked questions about Alien: Isolation's gameplay, features, and storyline.

Alien: Isolation is a survival horror game set 15 years after the events of the original Alien film. As Amanda Ripley, daughter of Ellen Ripley, you must investigate the disappearance of your mother on the remote space station Sevastopol.
01. What kind of gameplay can I expect?

Alien: Isolation combines stealth gameplay, crafting, and exploration with a strong focus on survival horror elements. You'll need to use your wits and resources to evade the Alien and uncover the truth about your mother's disappearance.

02. How realistic is the game?

The game is designed to be a realistic representation of survival horror, with a focus on creating a terrifying atmosphere and challenging gameplay. However, some creative liberties have been taken to enhance the gameplay experience.

03. What are the different types of enemies I'll encounter?

You'll encounter the Alien, as well as other hostile humans and androids. Each type of enemy will require different strategies and approaches to defeat or evade.

04. How long is the game?

A complete playthrough of the game typically takes 15-20 hours. However, the game's multiple endings and different possible outcomes encourage multiple playthroughs to explore various strategies and see different storylines and consequences of your decisions.
